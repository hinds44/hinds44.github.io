About

We are always looking for new people to become part of our family. Our current roster includes people who have been playing for 10+ years, and some who have never seen a MajorLeague Baseball game. If you fit between these two ends of the spectrum, you will be welcomed with open arms.

To organise ourselves, the coaches haved asked each member and potential member to join the app TeamStuff. This is where we update you with where and when training sessions and games are, and you tell us when you are available.
